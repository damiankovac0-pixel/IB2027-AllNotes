# A3.4 Alternative Databases And Data Warehouses

This is an HL topic.

This section extends databases beyond traditional relational systems. It covers alternative database models, data warehouses, OLAP, data mining, and distributed databases.

## Core Idea

Relational databases are strong for structured transactional data, but not every data problem is best solved with related tables.

Alternative databases and data warehouses are used when systems need flexibility, scale, analytics, geographical distribution, or business intelligence.
